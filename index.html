About Me

Hair Journey

I was working in manufacturing when I decided I wanted to persue my dreams to be a hairstylist. I started my journey doing hair over 6 years ago at Mitchell Community College. Once I was out of school, I took the time in a commission salon to learn the skills that I carry with me today. Wanting to offer clients a better experience, I left to be my own boss! I now rent a chair at Full Glam Beauty Bar and work next to some of the best girls in the business. I am excited to continue to grow in my skills and to meet many more people in the community!

Products and Favorite Services

I use Wella Professional Color, and MoroccanOil for all styling products. There is always a stock of MoroccanOil products available for purchase at the salon.
My favorite services include lived-in colors, such as balayage, as well as traditional foil highlights!

Outside of the Salon

When I am not in the salon I am at home with my amazing husband, two cats, and two dogs. There is never a dull moment with our house full of animals!

Treatments

Fusion Plex - $30-$50

Fusion Plex is an in-salon treatment made by Wella. It is a protein + moisturizing treatment that infuses the hair with amino acids that are missing due to color, brushing, tangles. It is topped with a moisturizing mask to keep the hair soft. This treatment lasts 8+ weeks. Highly recommended for color clients. $55 - Stand alone with blow dry and style $30 - Added to color or haircut

Conditioning Treatment - $20-$40

Pack in the moisture with a conditioning treatment! I have 4 different products and will choose one based on your specific hair type. $45 - Stand alone with blow dry and style $20 - Added to color or haircut

Keratin Smoothing Treatment - $200+

Global Keratin Smoothing Treatment is a long lasting treatment for easier management of the hair. This 2 hour in-salon treatment makes the hair easier to blow dry smooth for 3-5 months (depending on how often you shampoo). While it does smooth and decrease tangles and frizz, it does not completely straighten the hair - so you can still wear your hair curly should you wish! The treatment packs your hair with proteins which help with breakage in the long run. It is also progressive, so you will see better results with each treatment you get. Starting at $200 - increases depending on the hair thickness and length
